Car Wash Fundraising Made Simple

Raise funds for your organization effortlessly with Mr Sudz Car Wash. Here's how our fundraising program works:

1

Sign Up

Contact Mr Sudz to register your organization for our fundraising program. It's quick, easy, and completely free to join.

2

Receive Tickets

Get your custom fundraising wash tickets to sell to supporters, family, and community members at a discounted rate.

3

Sell & Earn

Sell the tickets to your community. For every ticket sold, your organization earns funds to support your cause or project.

4

Track & Collect

Track your sales and collect your earnings. We provide ongoing support to help maximize your fundraising success.
