FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you really never close?

Yes. Mr. Sudz is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year — rain, snow, nights, weekends, and holidays.

Where are you located?

Mr. Sudz Car Wash is located at 5560 W Broad St, Columbus, Ohio 43228.

Is Mr. Sudz a touchless car wash?

Absolutely. Mr. Sudz uses 100% touchless technology. No brushes or cloth touch your vehicle, helping protect your paint from scratches and swirl marks.

Is the wash safe for all paint types and finishes?

Yes. Our touchless system uses high pressure water and professionally formulated detergents that are safe for clear coats, wraps, ceramic coatings, and modern paint finishes.

Will the wash scratch my car?

No. Because there is no physical contact with your vehicle, the risk of scratching is significantly reduced.

How is Mr. Sudz different from tunnel washes?

Unlike traditional tunnel washes that use brushes and spinning material, Mr. Sudz is fully touchless. This means less friction, reduced risk of paint damage, faster wash times, and 24/7 availability.

How long does a wash take?

Most washes take approximately 2–3 minutes.

Do I need an appointment?

No appointment is required. Simply drive in whenever you are ready.

Is there a vehicle height or size limit?

Yes. The maximum vehicle clearance is 8 feet 4 inches (8′4″).

Are there vehicle restrictions?

Oversized vehicles or vehicles with roof racks, ladders, or external accessories may not be eligible. Please follow posted signage for safety.

What is the Mr. Sudz Club membership?

The Sudz Club is a monthly membership that includes up to 15 touchless washes per month per vehicle, with no long term contracts.

What’s included with my Sudz Club membership?

Members receive 24/7 wash access, free self serve vacuums, and fast, efficient cleaning anytime.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes. You can cancel at any time. There are no cancellation fees or hidden terms.

Do you offer fleet wash programs?

Yes. Mr. Sudz offers custom fleet wash solutions for businesses of all sizes, using touchless cleaning to protect paint and decals.
