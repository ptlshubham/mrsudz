Membership Terms & Conditions

By enrolling in a Mr. Sudz Car Wash Membership ("Membership" or "Club"), | acknowledge that I have reviewed, understand, and agree to the following Terms & Conditions.

1. Membership Overview

  • Memberships are offered as monthly subscription-based wash plans.
  • Each membership includes up to 15 washes per month, per membership.
  • Unused washes do not roll over to future months.
  • Memberships are per vehicle, not per person, household, or family.

2. Vehicle-Specific Use

  • Each membership is issued to one specific registered vehicle and is tied to a single license plate using license plate recognition technology.
  • Only the registered vehicle is authorized to use the membership.
  • Memberships may not be shared, transferred, or used on multiple vehicles, including vehicles within the same household or family.
  • Any use of a membership on an unregistered vehicle is considered misuse.
  • Misuse may result in temporary lockout, additional charges, or suspension or cancellation of the membership without refund.

3. Billing & Automatic Renewal

  • Memberships are automatically billed monthly to the payment method on file.
  • Memberships renew automatically each billing cycle until canceled.
  • Failed, declined, or disputed payments may result in temporary suspension of wash access until payment is resolved.

4. Cancellation Policy

  • Memberships may be canceled at any time through the app or online customer portal.
  • No refunds or partial credits are provided for unused washes.
  • Cancellation becomes effective at the end of the current billing cycle.

5. Wash Availability

  • Wash availability is subject to weather conditions, equipment maintenance, power outages, or safety concerns.

  • No refunds or credits will be issued for closures or downtime.
